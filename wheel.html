Wheel Diameter

Size (in mm) Functionality
50-53 mm Usually for tricks on street, park, and bowl; slower and performs poorly on rough ground
54-59 mm Average wheel size and used for street, park, bowl, and vert; faster
60+ mm Seen on longboards, old school boards, downhill boards; fastest and handles rough ground

Durometer

Durometer refers to the measured hardness of the wheel. Wheels come in the range of 78a-103a. When
choosing the wheel durometer, consider how you plan to skate the most - as wheel hardness affects the ride greatly.
