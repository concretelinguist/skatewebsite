Ben Raemers Tribute

Who was Ben Raemers?

Ben Raemers was a British skateboarder who rode for Enjoi Skateboard Co[0].

What brands did Raemers ride for prior to joining Enjoi?

Raemers rode for Consolidated Skateboards and eS Footwear previously. While he rode for Enjoi, he was also a part of Independent Trucks, Volcom, and Cons.

Timeline of Ben Raemers's life:

  • November 4, 1990: Ben Raemers was born in Colchester, Essex, England.
  • At age 10, he began skateboarding.
  • 2009: Enjoi recruited Raemers as an amateur team rider and introduced him in the Fall 09' feature video
  • December 17,2012: Enjoi released an 18-minute video titled "Tweak the Beef"[1], featuring several new and seasoned riders, including Raemers.
  • 2016: He became a cast member of King of the Road skateboarding contest hosted by Thrasher, one of the most distinguished skateboarding magazines.
  • May 14, 2019: Raemers passed away by suicide at the age of 28. Rest in peace. ✝
  • The Ben Raemers Foundation [2], an organization created to end mental health stigma by increasing public awareness, was established following his death

How Raemers impacted my life

I remember searching for youtube tutorials on a trick called the "boneless". I came across Ben Raemer's tutorial and he seemed to have an easy-going attitude and a good sense of humor. While practicing my boneless, I would refer back to his video. His flow made everything look smooth and easy.
