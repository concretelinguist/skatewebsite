Ben Raemers

Who was Ben Raemers?

Ben Raemers was a British skateboarder who rode for Enjoy Skateboard Co.

What brands did Raemers ride for prior to joining Enjoi?

Raemers rode for Consolidated Skateboards and eS Footwear previously. While he rode for Enjoi, he was also a part of Independent Trucks, Volcom, and Cons.

Timeline of Ben Raemers's life:

  • November 4, 1990: Ben Raemers was born in Colchester, Essex, England.
  • At age 10, he began skateboarding.
  • 2009: Enjoi recruited Raemers as an amateur team rider and introduced him in the Fall 09' feature video
  • December 17,2012: Enjoi released an 18-minute video titled "Tweak the Beef", featuring several new and seasoned riders, including Raemers.
  • 2016: He became a cast member of King of the Road skateboarding contest hosted by Thrasher, one of the most distinguished skateboarding magazines.
  • May 14, 2019: Raemers passed away by suicide at the age of 28.
  • The Ben Raemers Foundation, an organization created to end mental health stigma by increasing public awareness, was established following his death
